Privacy Policy for MORPH — Word Morph Puzzle

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Oilroot Labs ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the MORPH — Word Morph Puzzle mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

MORPH is a word puzzle game. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifying information directly. The App stores game progress (streak count, hints balance, puzzle history) locally on your device only.

Advertising

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including device identifiers and usage data. You can learn more about Google's data practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (hint packs and ad removal) processed through Google Play In-App Billing. We do not collect or store your payment information. All transactions are handled by Google Play.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools provided by Google to understand how users interact with our App. This data is anonymous and used only to improve the App experience.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Game progress data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal data is collected, the right to delete personal data, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal data. We do not sell your personal data. For any requests, contact us at the email below.

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA)

We agree to the CalOPPA requirements and will notify users of any privacy policy changes by updating this page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
